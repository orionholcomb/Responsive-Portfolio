About Me

My name is Orion Holcomb, and I was born in Lansing, Michigan where I didn't do very much before moving away from my homeland. At about 6 years old, me and my family moved out to Los Angeles, California and I pretty much grew up all my life around there. In the southern parts of Cali there happened to be an abundance of mexican immigrants, so I grew up eating conchas for breakfast and tamales for dinner. Over the years I began to develop a rather large imagination and I grew up writing stories and creating entire worlds inside my head and on paper. By the time I graduated middle school, we moved again.

In 9th grade I moved to Georgia, where I grew to love gaming! Every single day I would get home, drop my bags, and play games until I passed out. This made it very hard for me to socialize and complete important work, which really did a number on my relationship with my mother as well. I learned to manage my time and to be able to communicate with others, as this was a huge challenge for me in the 10th grade. One day, something clicked.

In the summer of 10th and 11th grade, I began to grow as a person more than I ever had before. I had to deal with summer school and it made me realize that I'm not invulnerable to inevitable progress and that it's time to become ready for my future life post-graduation. I started looking for colleges, but I couldn't find anything that stood out for me. After searching long and hard I thought about pursuing the one thing I love the most. What if instead of wasting my time gaming, I could make it my career? So I decided to pursue it, and nobody could stop me. Here I stand with my portfolio, which continues to grow each day as I complete new assignments and personal projects. The future is what I make of it.
